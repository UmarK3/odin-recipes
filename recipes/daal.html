Delicious Daal Chawal

Daal Chawal is a humble dish consisting of simple, steamed rice (chawal) paired with a nutritious lentil curry (dal), which is typically simmered with spices and often finished with a tempering (Tadka) of hot oil or ghee and whole spices. Culturally, this dish is far more than just food â€” it is the quintessential "ghar ka khana" (home-cooked food) across the Indian subcontinent.

Steps

Part 1: Chawal
  1. Rinse Rice - until water is clear
  2. Cook Rice - bring the water to a boil and cover the lid when reducing heat to low, and allow to simmer for 15 mintues.
  3. Reast & Fluff - Turn off the heat and allow the rice to rest for 10 mintues
Part 2: Daal(Lentils)
  1. Soak & Cook - rinse mixed daals, Cook the daal with water and tumeric inside a pressure cooker for 15 minutes
  2. Saute Spices - in a sepereate pan, heat up the Ghee/Oil and add cumin seeds, onions, ginger, garlic and green chilli unitl golden Brown then add hing, tomatoes and all powerdered spices. Cook until the paste thickens
  3. Combine & Simmer - combine lentils with spiced paste and add water to reach the desired consistency, stir and simmer for 5 mintues for flavours to blend
  4. Finish - stir in fresh hopped corriander and serve Hot daal over the chawal
